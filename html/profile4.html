Rashad Hussain

An American attorney who has served as US Special Envoy to the Organisation of Islamic Cooperation (OIC), the second largest intergovernmental body after the UN, with 57 member states,[1] and as US Special Envoy for Strategic Counterterrorism Communications. Hussain, a Muslim of Indian heritage, has also served in the White House Counsel's Office and on the National Security Council as a political appointee of the Obama Administration.
